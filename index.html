Volution: Field Recordings

2024

Packaging and design for Volution: Field Recordings, a 10” vinyl that contains eight experimental tracks, accompanied by a digital landscape.

The album explores the spiral as a symbol that stands in the place of ideas that lie beyond the reach of language.

Constituent of Everything

2025

30 prompt cards, made as prompts for a participatory audio performance. Seated in a circle around a recording device, participants were asked to embody their given prompt while breathing over the lip of a bottle. Designed and produced with Aiyana Alzamora.

Darkness Alive

2025

Three collecting cards with microscopic images of a speculative species; parasitic life forms of darkness.

What might it look like if a researcher were to try and document mystery? You might end up with things like collectible cards, completely ineffective in their documentation. Black, blurry images, of nothing, essentially.
