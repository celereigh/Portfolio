Celine Fu


Graphic designer and visual artist based in Toronto, ON.

Her work is centred by that which you regularly interact with but do not notice. Things that are real and unseen. Currently, this manifests as a fascination with graphic design as a tool to fabricate authority, and with irony as a means to subvert it.

Volution: Field Recordings

2024

Volution: Field Recordings contains eight experimental tracks, accompanied by a digital landscape. The album explores the spiral as a symbol that stands in the place of ideas that lie beyond the reach of language. The eternally nameless, the ever-evolving, who’s ultimate essence is impossible to describe yet evident in one’s being of aliveness.
